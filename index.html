OUR SERVICES

Precision CNC Machining Services

We specialize in high-precision CNC machining services for industries requiring tight tolerances and exceptional quality.

CNC Milling

High-precision 3-5 axis milling with tolerances down to ±0.0005" for complex geometries and prototypes.

  • 3, 4 & 5-axis capabilities
  • Aluminum, steel, titanium, plastics
  • Prototype to production runs

CNC Turning

Precision lathe work with live tooling for complex turned parts and high-volume production.

  • Swiss-style and multi-axis turning
  • Bar diameters from 0.020" to 3"
  • Secondary operations included

EDM Machining

Electrical Discharge Machining for hard metals and complex shapes that conventional machining can't handle.

  • Wire and sinker EDM capabilities
  • Tolerances to ±0.0001"
  • Hardened steels up to 65 HRC
